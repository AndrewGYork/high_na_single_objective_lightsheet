(untested) Parts list - emmission path

This table can be used as a shopping list for the current (untested) design of the emission path. For clarity, the elements are listed in order in which they are physically arranged from the sample to the camera. All of the optics are 'stock' parts except for items 10 and 11 which we are currently 'bespoke' and we are hoping to convert to stock moving forward. Item 10 can easily be substituted for a standard coverslip. If you are interested in item 11, the 'frustum' objective, then please contact us directly amsikking+highNAlightsheet@calicolabs.com.
