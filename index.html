Abstract

This research article is a work in progress - please check back in with us regularly to see updates. Eventually this article will describe (at least) how to design and build a bolt on module that converts a typical microscope into a high numerical aperture light sheet instrument that uses only one objective at the sample. This makes the microscope compatible with most traditional sample prepeartion methods like slides, dishes and multiwell plates. We hope the result will be relatively low cost and simple to build and align. We expect the performance to be fast and gentle high resolution volumentric imaging for anyone who wants to build it. We intend it to be 'game changer' in the world of live cell imaging...
